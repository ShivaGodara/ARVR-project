India's COVID-19 Vaccination Drive 2021

The world's largest vaccination campaign! India administered over 2 billion COVID-19 vaccine doses, demonstrating unprecedented healthcare logistics and planning capabilities.

Historic Achievement

Starting on January 16, 2021, India launched the world's largest vaccination drive, covering a population of 1.4 billion people across urban and rural areas.

Vaccination Strategy

India's phased approach prioritized healthcare workers, frontline workers, elderly citizens, and those with comorbidities. The drive utilized both Covishield and Covaxin vaccines manufactured domestically.

Digital Innovation

The CoWIN platform revolutionized vaccine distribution with real-time tracking, appointment scheduling, and digital certificates. Over 900 million people registered on the platform.

Global Impact

India's vaccination success became a model for the world, showcasing efficient healthcare delivery at scale. The country also exported vaccines to over 95 nations under the Vaccine Maitri initiative.

☀️ India's Solar Power Revolution

India has emerged as a global leader in renewable energy, with solar power at the forefront of this transformation. The country aims to achieve 500 GW of renewable energy capacity by 2030.

Solar Mission Overview

The National Solar Mission, launched in 2010, has transformed India into one of the world's largest solar markets. From just 2.6 GW in 2014 to over 70 GW today.

Major Solar Parks

India hosts some of the world's largest solar installations, including the Bhadla Solar Park in Rajasthan (2,245 MW) and Pavagada Solar Park in Karnataka (2,050 MW).

Technology & Innovation

India has developed indigenous solar manufacturing capabilities and innovative financing models, making solar energy accessible and affordable for millions of households and businesses.

Environmental Impact

Solar power has helped India avoid over 50 million tons of CO2 emissions annually, contributing significantly to global climate goals and sustainable development.
